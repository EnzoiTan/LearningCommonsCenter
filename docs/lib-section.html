LIBRARY SECTION

Circulation Section

This section contains multiple copies of books that can be used by students and faculty freely on the open shelves and can be borrowed for overnight and home reading.

Filipiniana Section

This section contains books written by a Filipino author, printed and published in the Philippines, or books about the Philippines.

Maritime Section

This section contains books about maritime education. This collection can be borrowed for overnight and home reading

Periodical Section

This section contains periodicals such as magazines, journals, newspapers, pamphlets, leaflets, etc. All materials are for library use only.

Reserve Section

This section contains in-demand books with limited copies currently used by the faculty members. Reserve collections are books assigned by the faculty members/librarians as references and addiotional readings.

E-Lib Section

This section is accessible to all users to guarantee quick service delivery through computer technolgy, online searching, and online databases. This E-Library service is on a "first come, first served" basis.
